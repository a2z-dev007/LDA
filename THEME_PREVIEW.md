## How to Switch

1. Open `src/theme/colors.ts`
2. Change line 10:
   ```typescript
   const ACTIVE_THEME: ThemeName = 'luxePinkGold';
   ```
3. Save and reload app
4. Done! 🎉

## Theme Switching Demo Script

**For Client Presentation:**

1. Start with **Elegant Dark** (current)
   - "This is our sophisticated dark theme"

2. Switch to **Luxe Pink & Gold**
   - "Now watch as we switch to match your logo..."
   - Change one line, reload
   - "Notice the gold headings and glowing pink buttons"

3. Show **Romantic Rose Gold**
   - "For a softer, warmer feel..."

4. Show **Midnight Passion**
   - "Or for maximum impact and energy..."

5. Return to **Luxe Pink & Gold**
   - "We recommend this one - it matches your brand perfectly"

**Impact:** Shows flexibility and attention to brand identity! 🎯
